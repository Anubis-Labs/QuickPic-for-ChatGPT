Settings & Configuration
Screenshot Method
Captures only the visible part of the current browser tab. This is the simplest method but requires you to be on the tab you want to capture.
Lets you select your entire screen, a specific application window, or another browser tab to capture. Gives you more flexibility to capture anything on your computer.
Use your operating system's screenshot tool (like Win+Shift+S on Windows), then the extension will automatically upload the image from your clipboard to ChatGPT.
General Settings
Display notifications when taking and uploading screenshots. This helps you know when actions are completed or if there are any errors.
Automatically close the popup after taking a screenshot. This streamlines your workflow by closing the extension popup after capturing.
